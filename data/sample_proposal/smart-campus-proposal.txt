Proposal Response: Smart Campus Security and Visitor Management Platform

Our platform provides secure visitor registration, host notifications, and centralized access logs for multi-site organizations.

Access and user controls
The system includes role-based permissions for administrators, front-desk staff, and security team members.

Auditability and reporting
Every visitor event is recorded with timestamped audit history. Standard dashboards include daily operational summaries and monthly activity reporting. Reports can be exported to CSV for compliance and internal review.

Implementation and onboarding
We propose an implementation timeline of 6 weeks from kickoff. The engagement includes onboarding workshops and administrator training for up to 25 staff members.

Data management
Our standard deployment includes nightly backups and a configurable data retention policy documented during implementation.

Support and references
This proposal includes a project timeline, support approach, pricing summary, and two education-sector case studies.
