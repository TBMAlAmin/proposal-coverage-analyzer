Request for Proposal: Smart Campus Security and Visitor Management Platform

1. Overview
The university seeks a vendor to provide a cloud-based platform for campus visitor management, access logging, and security reporting across three campuses.

2. Mandatory Requirements
The proposed solution must support role-based access control for administrators, reception staff, and security officers.
The vendor shall provide an audit trail for all visitor check-in and check-out events.
The system must generate daily and monthly security activity reports.
The solution must support exporting reports in CSV format.
The vendor must provide implementation within 8 weeks of contract award.
The bidder shall include onboarding and training for up to 25 staff members.
The proposal must describe the vendor's data retention policy and backup procedures.
The solution should provide optional SMS or email notifications for host alerts.

3. Submission Requirements
The proposal must include a delivery timeline, support model, pricing summary, and at least two relevant case studies.
